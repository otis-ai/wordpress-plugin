Launch data-driven digital ad campaigns on Facebook, Instagram and Google in 5 minutes

== Description ==

Launch data-driven digital ad campaigns on Facebook, Instagram and Google in 5 minutes. 

Our AI-powered mobile and web apps are designed for businesses who strive to connect with customers and generate results.

Otis is an all-in-one solution that makes digital marketing simple and accessible to launch and manage cross-channel ad campaigns using industry best practices to help you achieve 2x-3x better results, regardless of your experience level.

**Here’s how it works:**

- First, sync your data-sources or upload your existing customer list in the app settings. With the help of this data, over time, Otis will learn which ads are doing best and deliver those ads to your customers while finding new customers similar to them. This helps make targeting more effective and improve performance.
- Second, start creating your campaigns by selecting the social platforms you want to advertise on, customize the location and audience you want to reach. Then, choose a high quality image, write captivating ad copy, and schedule your campaign.
- Finally, track your results in the app to see how your campaigns are performing. While you’re busy focusing on your work, Otis dynamically adjusts your budget to improve your performance and provides AI recommendations.

== Installation ==

= Search =

The easiest way to install this plugin is to use the WordPress Admin interface.

- Go to your admin dashboard
- Find the "Plugins" menu
- Click on "Add New"
- Search for "Otis"
- Click "Install Now"
- Go to your "Plugins" menu
- Click "Activate" on the Otis - Digital Marketing plugin
- Approve to enable integration of your WordPress site with Otis
- Sign Up or Login to complete the process

Your WordPress site and Otis account will be connected and you're ready to get started with Otis.

= Upload =

1. Upload the 'otis' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
